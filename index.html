About Us

ED restaurant originally the vision of two restaurant nerds who were unsatisfied with the quality of the eropa food in their neighborhood. What started as a pop-up restaurant stall has transformed into a full restaurant with seating for over 50 guests

Our aim isn’t just to give people a place to meet and eating food, but to get people excited about the restaurant , and where it comes from. Got more questions? Drop by and join us for a chat.
